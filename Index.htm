Smallholding

Smallholding SRL is a private investment vehicle which holds participations in venture capital funds and startups.
We're looking to deploy capital in companies and initiatives that bring about the future we believe in: one of inclusion, kindness, equality, consciousness, acceptance and caring for all other living beings and for our planet.

All new investments are exclusively made with a Planet+ investment thesis.
